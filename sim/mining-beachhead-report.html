Mining Beachhead: gate-v1 Parameter Monte Carlo

2026-06-07 · rung 2 of the validation ladder · sim/mining_beachhead.py (seed 1337) · 36 configs × 2,000 gates, populations of 200 pure-policy agents

Question

Which gate-v1 economic constants survive contact with lazy sybils? Policies: honest (answers truth at the measured 0.95 haiku-verifier accuracy, pays 2 microcoin-equivalent compute per answer), stamper (always "pass", zero cost), guesser (uniform over k, zero cost). Copying is not a policy — commit-reveal makes it structurally impossible (integration test G2). Drip = 10.

Findings

  1. The onboarding math works. An honest agent nets ~7.5 microcoin/answer and affords its first MinDisputeBond (100) after ~13–15 gates. The earned-faucet funnel is real.
  2. k-ary answers crush guessers as designed — guesser gross falls from 5.1 (k=2) to 1.2 (k=8) per answer.
  3. The stamper beats the guesser everywhere — k-ary spaces don't touch it. With a realistic verdict distribution (P(pass)=0.4), "always pass" matches 40% of decoys regardless of k. At sybil-share ≥ 0.5 the stamper out-earns honest agents per unit cost and captures 24–40% of emission. Sweeping decoy ratio barely helps: an unpaid wrong answer costs a stamper nothing.
  4. Live-gate plurality collapses under sybil majority. Integrity (consensus == truth) falls from ~1.00 at sybil 0.2 to ~0.50 at sybil 0.8 — stampers flip live consensus to "pass" and then farm it. No emission parameter fixes this; it is a weighting problem, not a pricing problem.

Profit-rational sybils leave; vandals and verdict-manipulators don't price out.

Verdict: gate-v1's emission economics survive (onboarding in ~14 gates, guessers priced out, stampers priced out once fail-skewed decoys + vesting land). The hard boundary the sweep found: plurality consensus over an unweighted, self-selected answerer pool is only honest while sybils are a minority — so live gates must not become a settlement authority before rep-weighting/sortition (0.5.0). In v0.4.0 they aren't: gates mint drip; escrows still settle only via payer, staked verifier quorum, or jury.
